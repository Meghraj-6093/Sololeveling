Sung Jin-Woo's Shadows

Jin-Woo summons a powerful army of shadows from the beings he defeats. These include:

  • Shadow Soldier - One of the first shadows Jin-Woo commands.
  • Shadow Assassin - A stealthy and deadly shadow under Jin-Woo's command.
  • Shadow Mage - A powerful mage with immense magical abilities.
  • Shadow Monarch - The most powerful of all, who commands the entire army.
